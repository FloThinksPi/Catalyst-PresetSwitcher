What does this do ?

This App eneables you to switch your Catalyst Control Center Presets Remotely and eneables you e.g. to switch your main monitor to your connected tv and back to your normal monitors within seconds , without clicking trough menus. I Created this app to solve a small Problem , I often play Steamgames on my TV with a gamepad , therefore I have to change the main monitor to my tv , no problem but my tv is out of sight from my mouse and keyboard , so i was unable to switch back because i could not see anything , one tap on my smartphone solves it now.

How to Use?

Firstly you have to create some Presets , important is to keep the checkboxes under the tab Location in the new Preset dialog Checked! Secondly the App needs the Server Programm running on the target Windows Computer, additionally both devices(PC and Phone) need to be in the same Network. Port Forwarding is not recomendet because this software does not offer any security features.

Requirements:
-JRE7 Installed
-Windows Machine
-AMD Graphics Card with Catalyst Controll Center Installed
-Android Phone(JellyBean or newer)
-Same Network for Phone and target machine

!!Catalyst Presets often do not work for some Reason , if they dont work on your pc manually , they wont also work remotely with your phone so test them!!
